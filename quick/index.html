    Quick introduction

    One assesment methodology to rule them all

    In order to obtain a holistic view of degree of business performance, a universal assessment methodology is needed. The ITL performance system can help you to create science baked up methodology approach to measure and grade performance of your business system. In this way, you can analytically track and measure progress of business KPI-s and make corrective acts to improve your business KPI-s.

    Easy to learn even for non-professionals

    ITL-s syntax is specially designed to be easy to learn for non-professionals. In the design process, the state of the art user experience methodology was used.

    Powerfull graphic reports and easy to grasp performance rate

    People are visual. So, in order to maximise the power of business reporting, we made an easy and powerful graphic reporting system so you can easily understand the results of assessments.

    Backed up with science

    This project was the result of research, adaptation and implementation of the models created by the scientific research conducted by Omogbai, Farzad and Pakdil.
